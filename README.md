**Soundproof, Central AC Halls**
Stay perfectly cool. Shree Krishna Banquets features 100% centralized AC in both of our large halls. Plus, both halls are completely soundproof so your party stays private.

**Free Guest Changing Rooms**
We offer complimentary (free) changing rooms for the bride and the groom to get ready in peace. Also, each banquet floor comes with one extra male and one extra female changing room.

Your comfort matters. Each floor has very clean, separate bathrooms for ladies and gents, along with a separate handwash area.

**Huge Parking Space**
Do not worry about parking! We offer smooth valet parking for 50 to 60 cars and 100 to 150 two-wheelers. Safe street parking is also available right outside.

**Smart Lifts for Comfort**
Nobody likes crowded elevators. We have separate lifts for our guests and a different lift for the catering staff. This means no messy food carts will ever get in your guests' way.

**Full Power Backup**
We will never let a power cut spoil your special moments. We have a strong generator backup for the entire building. The lights, AC, and music will stay on!

**24/7 CCTV Security**
You and your guests are always safe. The whole banquet hall and the entire building area are fully watched by CCTV cameras.
